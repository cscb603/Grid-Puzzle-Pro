### **[中文]**

**照片太多拼不全？布局太死板？这个工具让拼图变简单。**

**星TAP | 智能网格拼图 (Grid Puzzle Pro)** 是一款专为摄影师、社交媒体达人和排版爱好者设计的智能拼图工具。不同于市面上那些死板的宫格模板，它支持**自由曲线切割**与**智能网格对齐**，能瞬间把你的一堆照片变成极具设计感的艺术作品。

- **✅ 自由拼图**: 突破传统的九宫格限制，支持自由切割和灵活布局，每一张拼图都独一无二。
- **✅ 智能对齐**: 内置智能算法，自动优化照片排布，确保多张照片在同一画框内比例和谐。
- **✅ 极简操作**: 小白也能上手，拖拽照片、选择模板、一键导出，告别复杂的 PS 操作。
- **✅ 绿色免安装**: 单文件便携版，下载解压即用，不写注册表，不占后台。

#### **如何使用 (Win 版)**
1. 在 [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/cscb603/Grid-Puzzle-Pro/releases) 下载 Grid_Puzzle_Pro_v1.0.0_Win_Portable.zip。
2. 解压到任意文件夹。
3. 双击 星TAP智能拼图V3.1.exe 即可开始创作。

### **[English]**

**Too many photos to fit? Tired of rigid layouts? Make collages effortlessly.**

**Grid Puzzle Pro** is an intelligent photo tiling and layout tool designed for photographers and creative users. It breaks away from traditional grid templates by offering **flexible curve cutting** and **smart grid alignment**, turning your photo collection into a professionally designed masterpiece in seconds.

- **Key Features**:
  - **Dynamic Layouts**: Beyond simple grids, it supports custom cutting and flexible arrangements for unique results.
  - **Smart Optimization**: Automatically balances photo proportions within the frame for a harmonious look.
  - **Ultra Simple**: Drag-and-drop workflow—perfect for users who want high-end results without complex software.
  - **Portable & Clean**: No installation required; run the single executable anywhere.

#### **Quick Start**
1. Download Grid_Puzzle_Pro_v1.0.0_Win_Portable.zip from [Releases](https://github.com/cscb603/Grid-Puzzle-Pro/releases).
2. Extract the archive.
3. Run Grid_Puzzle_Pro.exe.
